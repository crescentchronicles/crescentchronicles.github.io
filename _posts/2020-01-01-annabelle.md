Annabelle is the [Toreador](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Toreador) [Primogen](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Camarilla#Primogen) of Chicago infamous for throwing lavish and exclusive parties.
# Plot
14 years before the events of the chronicle, Annabelle needed a contact with University of Chicago's student population. She [Blood Bonded](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Blood_Bond) [[Yvonne]], who was a student at the time.

Annabelle was suspected to have blackmailed [[The Coterie]], but this was ruled unlikely thanks to [[Wauneka]]'s information.
