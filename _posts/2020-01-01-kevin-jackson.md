Kevin Jackson is [Prince](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Camarilla#Prince) of Chicago.
# Plot
Prince Jackson met with [[The Coterie]] at the [[Succubus Club]], where he asked them to work with [[Damien]], his Sheriff, to bring in [[Claudi]], a [Masquerade](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Camarilla#The_First_Tradition:_The_Masquerade) breacher, "as a favor."

Prince Jackson was the first American Prince to allow the [Lasombra](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Lasombra) to enter the Camarilla, under the condition that each admittee kill their sire. [[Jade]] was the first Lasombra to to be admitted in this way.
