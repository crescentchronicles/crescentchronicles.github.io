Yvonne Steijic is a ghoul whose ex-domitor is [[Annabelle]]. Yvonne was [Blood Bonded](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Blood_Bond) to arrange parties and other social functions, despite her awkward demeanor.
# Plot
[[The Coterie]] met Yvonne at a [[Baby Chorus]] show. She asked for their help in reserving a cemetery for a party. The group successfully convinced a priest to let them throw a "wake" at the cemetery.

Later Yvonne informed the coterie that the priest was disciplined and drew suspicion from "some secret society", and that she'd been discarded by her domitor. After much sobbing and begging, [[Cressida]] took Yvonne as her own ghoul.

After [[Claire]] and [[Christopher]] joked about trading her away to [[Wauneka]] for information, she broke down and ditched The Coterie to go home.
