Damien is Sherriff of Chicago and the frontman of [[Baby Chorus]].
# Plot
Damien briefed [[The Coterie]] on [[Claudi]]'s crimes against the Camarilla. The Coterie quickly brought Claudi back to him in torpor. Damien then executed Claudi at Prince [[Kevin Jackson]]'s request.

Later, Damien asked The Coterie to bring in [[Niko Pauli]] for siring Claudi. They returned claiming they had killed him, which did not convince Damien. He decided not to press them further.
