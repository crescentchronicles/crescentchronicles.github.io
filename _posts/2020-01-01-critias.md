Critias is a [philosopher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critias) from Athens posing as a professor at U of Chicago. He is also the [Brujah](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Brujah) [Primogen](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Camarilla#Primogen) of Chicago.
# Plot
[[Damien]]. put [[The Coterie]] in contact with Critias during their search for [[Niko Pauli]]. They visited him at his office, where he pulled a file of every known Brujah in the Chicago area and gave them information on how to find him.
