Claudi was a [Thin-blood](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Thin-blood) and serial [Masquerade](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Camarilla#The_First_Tradition:_The_Masquerade) breacher who was not fully aware of his nature.
# Plot
Claudi was responsible for several murders in which he left the victim fully exsanguinated. The Camarilla quickly took notice, and Prince [[Kevin Jackson]] asked [[The Coterie]] to work with [[Damien]] to "take care of it." The Coterie apprehended Claudi at the [[Red No. 5]] and dragged him back to Damien, who promptly executed him.
