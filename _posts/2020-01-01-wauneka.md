Wauneka is a [Nosferatu](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Nosferatu) who lurks around industrial areas on the South Side.
# Plot
Wauneka briefly ran into [[The Coterie]] when they were tracking down [[Niko Pauli]], who was squatting in his [domain](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Coterie_Backgrounds_and_Merits#Domain). He gave them Niko's exact location to rid himself of the poacher.

Later, The Coterie met with him to gain information on who was blackmailing them. Wauneka told them the one spying on them was a strange-looking ghoul wrapped in thick clothes. He also reminded them that their spy was using [Obfuscate](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/), which is not a [Discipline](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Disciplines) most [Toreador](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Toreador) know. This meant [[Annabelle]] is most likely not the blackmailer. In exchange for this key information, Wauneka asked [[Claire]] to deliver a letter to [[Jason Newberry]].
