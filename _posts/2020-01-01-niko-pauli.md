Niko is [[Claudi]]'s [Anarch](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Anarch) sire.
# Plot
[[The Coterie]] was tasked by [[Damien]] with finding Niko and bringing him in for violating the [Third Tradition](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Camarilla#The_Third_Tradition:_The_Progeny). They tracked him down, where he explained that he used the Embrace to scam Claudi out of $20,000, claiming it was an experimental medical treatment. He agreed that if he was let go, he would never return to Chicago. The Coterie decided to let him go after seeing his use of [Potence](https://vtm.paradoxwikis.com/Potence).
